How to play

  1. Host creates a room and shares the code/link.
  2. Everyone joins and sets their display name.
  3. Host starts the round. Everyone gets a secret question (Imposter gets a different one).
  4. Each player answers by naming someone in the group.
  5. After submissions, the majority question and all answers are revealed.
  6. Discuss and vote for the Imposter. Scores update automatically.
Suggestions: use a timer, a chat, or random avatars. See developer instructions below.
