How to play

  1. Host creates a room and shares the code/link.
  2. Everyone joins and sets their display name (names must be unique).
  3. Host starts the round. Everyone gets a secret question (Imposter gets a different one).
  4. Each player selects someone and then clicks "Submit Answer".
  5. After all submissions, the majority question and answers are revealed.
  6. Discuss and select who you think the Imposter is, then click "Submit Guess".
  7. Once voting finishes, the host can start the next round using the Next Round button.
